Frequently Asked Questions

Which patients are included in my count?

Patients with the target diagnosis for this QI project where you are listed as the Encounter Provider. Only clinic and office visits are included. Patients must be alive at the time of the report. Contact the QI team if you have questions about your patient list.

What counts as a complete screening?

All configured screening components must be documented within the past 12 months. A patient counts as fully screened only when every component has a recorded result in the EMR.

How do I update missing data for a patient?

Order or document the missing component at the patient's next visit. Labs ordered in the outpatient setting will populate automatically. Use structured EMR fields — free-text notes do not count. Updates will be reflected in the next report.

How often is this report sent, and what period does it cover?

Reports are sent every two months. Each report reflects a snapshot of your entire active panel — not just patients seen in the reporting period. A patient counts as screened if all components are on file within the 12 months preceding the report date.
